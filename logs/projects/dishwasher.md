**Dishwasher** is a pilot screenplay that centers around Mark Riley, an antisocial teen under the thumb of a secret government agency, who finally breaks free. He wakes up, remembering himself, his friends, and what happened.

I applied to the [2026 Wisconsin Screenwriters' Symposium](https://screenwriters.commarts.wisc.edu/) with this screenplay.
